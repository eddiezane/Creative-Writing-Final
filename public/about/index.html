About

This blog is to serve as a sanctuary for people who have tried to learn how to program but were scared off as the learning curve ramped upwards. Each week a video will be posted that focuses on one basic aspect of programming. It is important to have a solid foundation of the ideals and pricipals that are shared across all languages. Videos posted will range in time from 1 to 2 minutes and be very specific with their learning goal. Inspiration for this site has come from code.org and Code Academy. If you feel that the rate which we are going at is too slow for you, then it might be in your best interest to check out on of them.
